Intro

Harshit Gupta - Professional Profile

I am Harshit Gupta, a dedicated and accomplished professional currently pursuing a Master's of Science in Computer Science at Indiana University, Bloomington. With a GPA of 3.67/4, I completed my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering from Thadomal Shahani Engineering College, Mumbai, where I excelled with a GPA of 8.62/10.

My expertise spans a wide range of programming languages, including C++, Python, Java, and R, coupled with proficiency in web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and React. I have a solid understanding of databases, working with SQL, MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and Snowflake. Additionally, I am well-versed in various tools like Excel, Docker, Tableau, AWS, and Jenkins.

My academic achievements include a publication on doodle recognition using ensemble learning and involvement in impactful projects such as Health Insurance Market Analysis, Credit Card Transactions Fraud Detection, Doodle Recognition, and Predictive Maintenance Using Deep Learning.

Beyond academics and work, I am a TSEC Hall of Fame winner, securing the 1st position in the 'Smart India Hackathon 2019.' I am also a proud member of the Ideation & Creation Entrepreneurs (ICE) Club at Luddy School of Informatics at Indiana University.

I am enthusiastic about leveraging my academic excellence, technical skills, and professional experiences to make significant contributions to the field of computer science.

Work

In my role as a Full Stack Developer at Mahindra & Mahindra Financial Services, I developed a comprehensive application dashboard for a nationwide customer leads management system. I scripted an internal chatbot to streamline HR-related queries, implemented Airflow for optimized report generation, and contributed to a significant increase in daily performance efficiency.

As a Software Development Engineer at Piramal Capital & Housing Finance, I designed an automated system for customer masking, developed a customer risk assessment scorecard model, and created an anomaly detection system for API management. My leadership skills were put to the test as I managed and guided a team of interns, ensuring successful project outcomes.

During my tenure as a Python Developer at Cogno AI, I deployed software applications for chatbots, revamped systems using JavaScript, and effectively administered client requirements with a focus on agile methodology.
